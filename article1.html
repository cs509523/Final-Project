  1. Cristiano Ronaldo

    The debate between Ronaldo and Messi on who the best player in the world is one that continues to grow. So far this season Ronaldo has proved to be the best goalscorer in Europe. Ronaldo has scored 10 goals so far this season. Although he is often over zealous in his approach, when it comes to pure skill, Ronaldo is second to none.

  2. Lionel Messi

    Messi, often given the title, as the best player of all time, has been having a great season. The little man from Argentina has scored 5 goals so far for FC Barcelona. Messi receives a great portion of his admiration from his style of the play. He is a great team player and never selfish with the ball. His left foot makes for arguably the most accurate strikes in all of soccer.

    The Dutch Striker, Arjen Robben, proved himself in the world cup to be one of the best goal scorers in the world.   5. Luis Suarez

    Luis Suarez was banned from play in the 2014 World Cup for biting an opponent. He has since been suspended for 3 months from all of soccer. Despite this controversy he is still one of the best strikers in the world. Suarez was able to find the back of the net twice in just two outings for Uruguay.

  6. Neymar Jr.

    The 22 year old Brazilian has certainly earned his place in any top 10 list after his performance in the World Cup. Neymar was able to score 4 goals in just 5 games. He is without a doubt the fastest player on the pitch. Neymar Jr has a rare ability to find a way to consistently get open in front of net.

  7. Gareth Bale

    The Welsh Midfield unfortunately was unable to participate in this past World Cup as his team failed to qualify. He has proven himself to be one of the best free kickers of all time. His unrivaled speed allows him to blow by defender and get in front of goal week after week.

  8. Robin Van Persie
